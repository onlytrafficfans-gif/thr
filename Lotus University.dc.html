Lotus University
COMPARE LOTUS UNIVERSITY ABOUT

Learn. Build. Launch.

Lotus University is the educational platform included with select Lotus packages. It is designed to help entrepreneurs, creators, developers, and business owners learn how to confidently build, customize, and grow with the Lotus platform.

Whether you're creating your first AI application or expanding an existing business, Lotus University provides structured lessons, practical walkthroughs, and real-world guidance that help you get the most from your investment.

Unlike traditional online courses, Lotus University is built specifically around the Lotus ecosystem. Every lesson is designed to help you understand not only how to use the platform, but why each tool exists and how it fits into building a successful business.

What You'll Learn

App Builder Fundamentals

Learn how to confidently use the Lotus App Builder to create, organize, and manage your own applications.

Prompt Engineering

Understand how to write effective prompts that produce better results from AI models and improve your workflow.

Business Development

Learn practical approaches for organizing your services, presenting your products, and building a professional digital presence.

AI Workflows

Explore ways AI can simplify repetitive tasks, improve productivity, and support your creative process.

Platform Walkthroughs

Step-by-step tutorials covering the major tools, features, and capabilities available inside the Lotus platform.

Best Practices

Learn recommended workflows, organization techniques, and strategies to help you work more efficiently as your projects grow.

Who It's For

Lotus University is designed for:

  • • Entrepreneurs
  • • Small business owners
  • • Developers
  • • Designers
  • • Content creators
  • • Freelancers
  • • Agencies
  • • Anyone looking to build with AI using the Lotus platform
